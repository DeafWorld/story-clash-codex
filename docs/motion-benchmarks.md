# Story Clash Motion Benchmark Notes

## Goal
Ship premium motion quality for a social narrative game while keeping a distinct visual identity.

## Quality Signals To Emulate
1. Fast first-impression animation clarity.
2. Consistent transition language across screens.
3. Strong state feedback for wins, danger, and major narrative beats.
4. Mobile-safe touch responsiveness during animation.

## Signals To Avoid
1. Copying competitor timing signatures one-to-one.
2. Reusing similar composition motifs or scene choreography.
3. Overusing high-frequency camera shake or unreadable overlays.

## Story Clash Motion Identity
1. Cinematic Rift background drift with layered parallax.
2. Deterministic pressure pulses coupled to director cues.
3. Short, decisive impact flashes on payoff/fracture beats.
4. Calm cooldown breaths after major spikes.

## Performance Guardrails
1. Prefer transform/opacity animations.
2. Keep expensive blur layers minimal.
3. Downshift effects under reduced-motion or low-performance conditions.
