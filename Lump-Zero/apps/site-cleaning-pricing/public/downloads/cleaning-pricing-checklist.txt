CLEANING PRICING CHECKLIST

1. Confirm square footage.
2. Count bedrooms and bathrooms.
3. Choose the actual service type.
4. Note frequency and whether a recurring discount applies.
5. Add fridge, oven, windows, or laundry extras only when requested.
6. Check travel time and access constraints.
7. Add a minimum visit price if the job is very small.
8. Copy or print the estimate summary before sending the quote.
