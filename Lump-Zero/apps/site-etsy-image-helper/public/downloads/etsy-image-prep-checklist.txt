ETSY IMAGE PREP CHECKLIST

1. Pick the Etsy surface first.
2. Start from the largest original available.
3. Check the target ratio in the Etsy image helper.
4. Keep the focal subject away from the edges.
5. Export at the recommended size for the selected preset.
6. Recheck the file after any crop-heavy edits.
7. Upload only after the source clears the quality warning.
